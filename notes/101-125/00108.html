"Greetings,

"Three weeks on from the publication of the original article in NATURE, we have over 11,000 enthusiastic punters, cautious but nevertheless remarkably positive support from the UK Met Office (perhaps I was unfair on peer-review committees), discussions going with IBM, BP, Apple and various software houses, and, last but by no means least, a lively debate in the scientific community as to how exactly the experiment should work.
