TEXAS RENEWABLE ENERGY ROUNDUP Green Living & Sustainability Fair UPDATE August 7, 2000

EARLY DISCOUNT DATE APPROACHING

Save a few bucks and reserve your space at the Roundup by August 15. It could mean an extra couple of bottles of great Texas wines, a few extra German pastries, or a splurge in one of the many shops and galleries in Fredericksburg.

SPEAKERS, SEMINARS AND WORKSHOPS TO BE ANNOUNCED SOON

Over 50 seminars and featured speakers plus some 20 specialized workshops will be announced on the Roundup website by mid-August. Be sure to check them out at www.RenewableEnergyRoundup.com.

ROUNDUP ADVERTISING CAMPAIGN BUILDING MOMENTUM

The Roundup advertising campaign includes:

  1. thousands of brochures and bookmarks circulated over the last several months;
  2. the www.RenewableEnergyRoundup.com web site linked to the Fredericksburg Convention and Visitors Bureau web site and many others;
  3. press releases to over 1,500 news outlets through the services of PR Newswire, and other media lists;
  4. ads in publications such as Texas Monthly, Home Power Magazine, Solar Today, Home Grown, and Natural Home Magazine;
  5. ads in assorted newspapers and weekly entertainment tabloids;
  6. calendar listings in dozens of magazines and newsletters;
  7. bill stuffers mailed to over 585,000 customers by City Public Service;
  8. hundreds of posters and thousands of flyers distributed in Austin, San Antonio, Houston, Dallas/Fort Worth, throughout the Hill Country and in other cities;
  9. mention of the Roundup on topically related radio shows in major markets;
  10. promotion through auspices of affinity groups such environmental and consumer groups.

Don't miss being a part of the Roundup. Exhibit spaces are still available. Select your space now. Complete the Exhibitor Application form found in the green and white Roundup Exhibitor Information brochure and send it in right away.

See you at the Roundup!

Russel Smith Roundup Organizer (512) 345-5446 R1346@aol.com
